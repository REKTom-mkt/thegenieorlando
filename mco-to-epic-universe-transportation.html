About Universal's Epic Universe

Epic Universe will be Universal Orlando's third theme park and largest to date. Opening May 22, 2025, the park represents a $6.5 billion investment and features five immersive worlds unlike anything Orlando has seen before:

  • Super Nintendo World
  • The Wizarding World of Harry Potter – Ministry of Magic
  • How to Train Your Dragon – Isle of Berk
  • Dark Universe
  • Celestial Park (central hub)

Located about 5 miles south of the existing Universal Orlando Resort, Epic Universe features three new on-site hotels for the ultimate immersive experience.

Epic Universe On-Site Hotels We Serve

The Genie provides direct private transportation from MCO to all three new Epic Universe resort hotels:

  • Universal Stella Nova Resort – Cosmic-themed resort with stellar amenities
  • Universal Terra Luna Resort – Earth and moon inspired design
  • Universal Helios Grand Hotel – Premium hotel with park views
Transfer Details
  • Distance from MCO: Approximately 25 miles
  • Drive Time: 30-45 minutes depending on traffic
  • Service Area: Park entrance and all on-site hotels
