About The Genie Transportation Services

Aviation-Level Safety Meets Disney-Standard Hospitality in Orlando

At a Glance

Founded by Simone Cerasa, a licensed FAA pilot and former Disney Cast Member, The Genie Transportation Services provides premier private transportation in Central Florida.

We specialize in:

  • Private MCO Airport Transfers
  • Luxury Disney World Resort Transportation
  • Premium Port Canaveral Cruise Transfers
  • Universal Studios transportation

By combining aviation-level safety standards with world-class hospitality, The Genie delivers a First Class ground transportation experience designed for families and discerning travelers.

A proud member of the Board of Directors of the Florida Limousine Association representing Central Florida.

From the Skies to the Streets: The Vision Behind The Genie

The Genie was born during a pivotal moment in modern travel history. Following the 2020 pandemic, the Central Florida transportation landscape changed dramatically. Recognizing a growing gap for reliable, professional service, Simone Cerasa founded The Genie Transportation Services with a clear mission: to bring First Class standards to Orlando ground transportation, rooted in safety, integrity, and the Disney way of service.

A Foundation in Disney Excellence

Simone’s commitment to exceptional guest experiences began in 2004 with the Walt Disney World College Program. During his time with Disney, he mastered Guestology principles at iconic locations including:

  • EPCOT – Test Track and Mission: SPACE
  • Disney Springs – The Candy Cauldron

This deep understanding of the Orlando theme park ecosystem allows The Genie to provide insider navigation and logistical guidance that traditional rideshare drivers simply cannot match.

Aviation-Grade Safety and Global Standards

Before launching The Genie, Simone spent nearly two decades as an International Purser for a major U.S. airline. In addition to his leadership background, Simone is a licensed FAA pilot—a credential that fundamentally shapes how The Genie operates.

How Aviation Safety Changes Your Private Transfer:

Aviation-Level Maintenance

Our vehicles are inspected with the same rigor required for aircraft.

Precision Logistics

We monitor flight arrivals and Orlando traffic patterns with the discipline of a flight dispatcher.

Safety First Culture

From professional car seat installation to every mile driven, safety is never negotiable.


At The Genie, passengers aren’t just customers—they’re precious cargo.

Industry Leadership and Local Advocacy

Simone currently serves as an elected member of the Florida Limousine Association Board of Directors, working alongside industry leaders to uphold professional transportation standards and advocate for passenger safety in the Orlando market.

Our Promise to Your Family

We believe your vacation doesn’t start at the hotel—it starts the moment you land at Orlando International Airport (MCO). As a family-owned business, we specialize in removing travel friction for parents by providing:

  • Professionally sanitized, high-quality car seats
  • A warm, professional meet-and-greet at baggage claim
  • Reliable, private transportation you can trust

Our Core Values: The Five Pillars of The Genie

Safety Above All (The Pilot’s Standard)

In aviation, there is no margin for error. We bring that same uncompromising mindset to the road through rigorous maintenance and precise car seat installation.

Service with the "Disney Touch"
