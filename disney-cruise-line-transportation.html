Disney Cruise Line Transportation from Orlando

Disney Cruise Transfers:
Start Your Voyage Stress-Free

Your magical Disney Cruise vacation deserves a magical start. The Genie Transportation provides premium private transfers to Port Canaveral for all Disney Cruise Line sailings, whether you're flying into Orlando International Airport (MCO) or starting from a Walt Disney World resort hotel.

Disney Cruise Line operates from Cruise Terminal 8 at Port Canaveral, offering voyages on the Disney Wish, Disney Fantasy, Disney Dream, Disney Magic, and the newest addition, Disney Treasure. Our drivers know exactly where to take you for a smooth embarkation experience with complimentary car seats for your little sailors.

With real-time flight monitoring and spacious luxury SUVs, the whole family travels safely and comfortably to begin your adventure at sea. We'll even help get your luggage to the porters at the terminal.

Disney Cruise Terminal Information

Disney Cruise Line operates exclusively from Cruise Terminal 8 (CT8) at Port Canaveral. This dedicated terminal features the same magical theming and attention to detail you'd expect from Disney, making embarkation an exciting part of your cruise experience.

  • Disney Wish
  • Disney Fantasy
  • Disney Dream
  • Disney Magic
  • Disney Treasure
  • Disney Destiny (Coming Soon)

The terminal is designed for efficient boarding with dedicated check-in areas, character meet-and-greets, and that first magical moment when you walk up the gangway and hear "Welcome aboard!"

Disney Cruise Transfer Details

The Genie provides direct private transportation from Orlando International Airport or Walt Disney World resorts to Port Canaveral for Disney Cruise Line:

  • Terminal: Cruise Terminal 8 (CT8)
  • Distance from MCO: Approximately 55 miles
  • Drive Time: 50-70 minutes depending on traffic
  • Pickup Options: MCO Airport or Disney Resort
What's Included

  • Complimentary child safety seats

  • Luggage assistance to terminal porters
