Premium Stroller Rentals
Delivered to You

When you book your round-trip transfer with us, simply add a premium Baby Jogger stroller to your reservation. It will be professionally sanitized, spotless, and ready for you in your vehicle upon pickup at MCO.

We provide single and double Baby Jogger City Mini GT2 and GT3 strollers, known for their lightweight, compact design, and easy maneuverability. These models adhere to all size regulations for Disney (Magic Kingdom, Epcot, Hollywood Studios, Animal Kingdom) and Universal Studios, ensuring smooth navigation through the parks.

Our strollers feature cozy reclining seats, spacious storage baskets (up to 10 lbs), and UV50+ canopies with peekaboo windows. Each rental includes a parent console, a rain cover, and a complimentary case of water to keep you refreshed.

Your driver will have the stroller ready at your initial pickup and will collect it upon your return journey, eliminating the need for separate delivery arrangements or scheduling.
