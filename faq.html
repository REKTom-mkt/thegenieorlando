Frequently Asked Questions: Orlando Airport, Disney & Port Canaveral Transportation

This page answers the most common questions about Orlando airport transportation, Disney World resort transfers, Port Canaveral cruise transportation, Universal Studios transportation, stroller rentals, safety, and service options with The Genie Transportation Services.


Arrival and Pickup Logistics at Orlando International Airport (MCO)

Your pickup location depends on the level of service you reserved:

Genie VIP Service or Meet & Greet Add-On

Your driver will meet you inside the airport at baggage claim, holding a sign with your name clearly displayed. This service includes full assistance with luggage from the carousel to the vehicle.

Genie XL Service

All Genie XL pickups occur on Level 1 (Ground Transportation) at designated outdoor pickup spaces:

  • Terminal A: Spaces A1–A4 or A42–A45
  • Terminal B: Spaces B1–B4 or B40–B41
  • Terminal C: After exiting the sliding doors, your driver will be waiting to the left in the ground transportation area.

Genie Express or Genie Comfort Service (8:00 AM to Midnight)

Pickups are coordinated by specific doors based on your airline and terminal:

  • Terminal A: * Spirit, Frontier, and Avelo: Proceed to Express Pickup Door 115 (Take elevator near baggage carousel 5 down to Level 1).
    • Southwest: Proceed to Express Pickup Door 145 (Take elevator near baggage carousel 13 down to Level 1).
  • Terminal B: * Delta, Sun Country, Allegiant, and WestJet: Proceed to Express Pickup Door 110 (Take elevator near baggage carousel 29 down to Level 1).
    • American, United, Breeze, Alaska, and Air Canada: Proceed to Express Pickup Door 140 (Take elevator near baggage carousel 23 down to Level 1).
  • Terminal C: * Proceed to Level 1; as you walk outside past the sliding doors, your driver will be waiting to the left in the ground transportation pickup area.

Genie Pro-Tip: MCO is one of the busiest airports in the country. Turn your phone on as soon as you land—your driver will text you their exact location and vehicle details to ensure a smooth connection.

The Genie monitors all inbound flights to MCO in real time. If your flight is delayed or cancelled by more than one hour, please notify us via text at 689-258-3572. We will coordinate your updated arrival as efficiently as possible.

Private Walt Disney World Resort Transportation & Hotel Transfers

The Genie provides private, direct Disney World transportation designed specifically for families.

  • No Multiple Stops: You go straight to your resort—no shared shuttle delays.
  • Family-First Experience: As members of the Family Travel Association, we assist with luggage and strollers to keep your family comfortable from the start.

Genie Pro-Tip: Shared shuttles often take 90+ minutes after multiple resort stops. With The Genie Transportation, most families reach their Disney World Resort within 35–45 minutes of leaving the airport.

We provide door-to-door transportation to all Disney World resort categories, including:

  • Deluxe Resorts: Grand Floridian, Polynesian Village, Contemporary, Animal Kingdom Lodge, Wilderness Lodge, Yacht & Beach Club, BoardWalk Inn.
  • Moderate Resorts: Riviera Resort, Coronado Springs (including Gran Destino Tower), Caribbean Beach, Port Orleans.
  • Value Resorts: Art of Animation, Pop Century, and all All-Star Resorts.
  • Other Disney-Area Properties: Four Seasons Orlando, Shades of Green, Swan, Dolphin, and Swan Reserve.

Port Canaveral Cruise Transportation

Yes. The Genie offers private transfers to and from all Port Canaveral cruise terminals, serving all major cruise lines including Disney Cruise Line, Royal Caribbean, Carnival, NCL, MSC, Celebrity, and Princess.

We provide door-to-door service to all major hotels in Cape Canaveral and Cocoa Beach, including: Radisson Resort at the Port, Homewood Suites, Hampton Inn & Suites, Residence Inn, and Westgate Cocoa Beach Pier Resort.

Exclusive Disney World Stroller Rentals

Yes. We offer premium Baby Jogger stroller rentals exclusively for guests who book round-trip transportation with The Genie Transportation.

There is no hotel delivery appointment or third-party coordination required. Your Disney-approved stroller is pre-loaded in your Genie vehicle at the airport, and at the end of your trip, you simply leave it in the vehicle during your return transfer.

We offer the premium Baby Jogger GT Series:

  • Single: City Mini GT2 or GT3
  • Double: City Mini GT2 Double
