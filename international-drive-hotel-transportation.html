About International Drive

International Drive (I-Drive) is Orlando's premier tourist corridor, stretching over 11 miles and offering endless entertainment options. It's perfectly located between Walt Disney World, Universal Orlando, and SeaWorld, making it an ideal home base for your Orlando vacation.

  • ICON Park & Orlando Eye
  • SeaWorld Orlando
  • Aquatica Water Park
  • Fun Spot America
  • Orange County Convention Center
  • Hundreds of Restaurants & Shops

The I-Drive Trolley provides convenient transportation along the corridor, but you'll want reliable private service to get from the airport to your hotel quickly and comfortably.

MCO to International Drive Details

The Genie provides direct private transportation from Orlando International Airport to any hotel or resort along International Drive:

  • Distance from MCO: 12-18 miles (varies by location)
  • Drive Time: 20-35 minutes depending on traffic
  • Coverage: All I-Drive hotels from Sand Lake to Destination Parkway
  • Theme Park Proximity: Minutes to Universal, SeaWorld, and Disney
