Royal Caribbean Cruise Transportation from Orlando

Royal Caribbean Transfers:
Your Adventure Awaits

Get ready for an incredible Royal Caribbean cruise! The Genie Transportation provides premium private transfers from Orlando International Airport (MCO) directly to Port Canaveral's cruise terminals, where Royal Caribbean operates some of the world's most impressive cruise ships, including the record-breaking Icon of the Seas.

Royal Caribbean primarily uses Cruise Terminal 1 (CT1) at Port Canaveral, with larger ships also utilizing the new Terminal A. Our drivers know exactly which terminal your ship departs from, getting your family there with time to spare and complimentary car seats for your children.

With spacious luxury SUVs, real-time flight monitoring, and meet & greet service inside MCO baggage claim, we make embarkation day stress-free so you can focus on the excitement ahead.

Royal Caribbean Transfer Details

The Genie provides direct private transportation from Orlando International Airport to Port Canaveral for Royal Caribbean cruise sailings:

  • Primary Terminal: Cruise Terminal 1 (CT1)
  • Larger Ships: Terminal A (Icon-class vessels)
  • Distance from MCO: Approximately 55 miles
  • Drive Time: 50-70 minutes depending on traffic
